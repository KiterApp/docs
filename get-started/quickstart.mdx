## Welcome to PromptLoop

PromptLoop empowers you with cutting-edge AI tools to automate and enhance your data tasks. Create tasks that can accomplish your work including research, web scraping, text analysis, and building out valuable company datasets.

### Key Features

- **Flexible**: Access hundreds of models and functions under a simple task-based editor.
- **Accurate**: Receive formatted and sourced answers from reliable models.
- **Transparent**: See exactly what your tasks are accomplishing and improve them with edits.

## Core Concepts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="list-check" title="Tasks">
    Tasks are actions for our systems to accomplish for you. They consist of inputs (like a website or search item) and return outputs as new columns or rows of data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="database" title="Datasets">
    Datasets are repositories for your information. You can search, filter, save, and share inputs and outputs from AI tasks up to hundreds of thousands of rows.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="table" title="API Endpoints">
    The PromptLoop API lets you launch and monitor jobs to enrich data and create Datasets
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Steps to Get Started

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="1. Define Your Goal" icon="bullseye-arrow">
    Create a new task using our extensive task library or custom editor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Test and Edit" icon="vial">
    Use the custom generated test page to preview outputs and make necessary edits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3. Run a Dataset" icon="play">
